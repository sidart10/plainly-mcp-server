### 1. Start Simple
Don't overcomplicate. A simple animated diagram works great.

### 2. Test First
Download, customize, test in After Effects before uploading to Plainly.

### 3. Mark Everything Dynamic
In Plainly, mark all text/colors as dynamic so you can change per video.

Design template to work for different use cases, not just one specific diagram.
